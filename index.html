This site uses the concepts of GIS to relate the range maps of the snake species in Texas to their locations within each county. Select your county and a list of snakes that are likely to be count there will be dispayed. Additionally, because no range map is guarenteed accurate, snakes that found in neighboring counties that aren't in the selected county will also be displayed.
